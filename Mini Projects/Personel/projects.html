DESIGN AND EXPERIMENT ANALYSIS OF
ROAD CLEANER WITH METAL COLLECTOR

INTRODUCTION

  • In recent years cleanliness is becoming an important factor for the betterment of the nation Effective cleaning and sanitizing helps and protect the health of the human beings directly and indirectly. It also improves the shelf life of the floor, walls etc due to regular cleaning and maintenance Vacuum cleaner is widely applied to eliminate spills at homely levels, but might fail to remove non flying metal spills, as it damages the tube of vacuum cleaner
  • There is no one single cleaning method that is suitable for all locations and occasions and effective cleaning depends upon type of cleaning device, cleaning technique and also the equipment should be user friendly. So far, people are no longer satisfied that road cleaning vehicles only perform single cleaning task. In this project it is aimed to cover over metal particle and solid waste to collect waste as well as separate the waste in same period of time
  • And to perform both smooth and rough surfaces like mud, wood, concrete, marbles, roads etc According to find the best cleaning brush for that particular surface, repeating cleaning process is occur In this work,easily available materials with low cost is used and it can be easily fabricated and easy to control. It is the better alternative for conventional machine

FABRICATION

  • The machine uses two separate rods, in this one is connected at the front of the machine and another one is connected at the back of the machine ,the rod one connected which is below having magnetic roller which having a function to collect metal waste and chips The magnetic chip collector consists of a set of permanent magnets arranged axially utilizing a shaft. The shaft has gears on front side of the wheel These gears are meshed together which are arranged coaxially to shafts
  • The rollers also have bearings for ease of rotation on which the belt moves and the bearings are supported by metal blocks which in turn welded to frame. The collector is designed as rectangular in shape made up of aluminum metal sheet that fit metal frame The collector is also designed which can be easily removed after filling and placed again, when it is empty
  • In another rod the roller brush is connected with help of bearing and it rotates with the help of pulley drive Two rolling brush setup is used for dust cleaner concept which consists of bearnings roller brush and rod And also dust collector is placed behind the brush to store the wastes The whole assembley is attached to the main frame which consists of handle to drive the vehicle

WORKING

  • In this project, it is attempted design and fabricate the manual operated sweeper with the help of chain drive. When the base frame is moved with the help of handle, the rear wheel will be rotated. The rotating wheels are connected to chain drive Chain drive is a way of transmitting mechanical power from one place to another. It passing over a sprocket gear,with the teeth of the gear meshing with the holes in the links of the chain
  • So that the chain drive connected with it will be rotated which will rotate the rear wheel. Simple gear mechanism encompose the magnetic roller to rotate reverse direction. It attracts the ferrous chip and it is carried out on the conveyor belt It is arranged inbetween the roller shaft and magnetic roller. A chip pass away from the magnetic roller,get demagnetize and falls in collector under the influence of gravity.
  • On other hand, the roller brush also has a pair of collector box used to sweep the waste and automatically stored in the collector box is made up of aluminium sheet with the help of manual power
  • Particularly in dust cleaning concepts, it is designed to change the brush setup For example, nylon brush to natural fibre brush Having two brush setup is designed to work different types of surface. Hence the sweeping brush and magnetic conveyor will be rotated which will help to collect the wastes over the area automatically. Both the collectors are filled with separate metal chips and dust particles are removed and make it empty for repeating process .
